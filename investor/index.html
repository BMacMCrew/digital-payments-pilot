The Problem We Solve

Payment Processing Takes Weeks

Manual approvals, paper-based workflows, and batch processing create delays that slow critical operations, especially in emergency response scenarios.

$175B in Improper Payments Annually

Lack of automated compliance checks, weak audit trails, and fragmented systems enable fraud, waste, and abuse across federal programs.

No Real-Time Visibility

Agencies can't track payment status, reconciliation is manual, and financial reporting is delayed by weeks or months.

Fragmented Payment Rails

Agencies struggle with multiple vendor requirements (ACH, wire, check, card) while Treasury mandates strict compliance that slows everything down.

The Actus Solution

Blockchain Orchestration Layer

  • Automates acquisition and financial compliance before payments execute
  • Generates SPRs (Standard Payment Requests) on blockchain with immutable audit trail
  • Ensures atomic settlements - all parties paid together or none paid
  • Integrates directly with Treasury BFS and Federal Reserve FedNow Service

Universal Payment Gateway

  • Single interface to all payment rails: FedNow, RTP, ACH, Fedwire, SWIFT
  • G2B virtual cards backed by stablecoin - no vendor onboarding required
  • Works with any ERP system (SAP, Oracle, Momentum, legacy systems)

Smart Contracts & Automation

  • Pre-programmed compliance rules eliminate manual reviews
  • 3-way match (PO, Invoice, Receipt) automated with CGAC/TAS validation
  • 95%+ transactions auto-processed in under 60 seconds
